Participating the Workshop

The workshop is open and free to anyone who is interested. In order to faclitate our workshop arrangement, registration is required prior to the event. Please fill in this registration form.

Registration deadline: September 10th, 2023.

If you are interested in being a science contributor to share the challenges you face when dealing with big data, please feel free to contact the organizer Dr. Zhonghua Zheng (zhonghua.zheng at manchester.ac.uk).

If you have any other questions, please contact organizer Dr. Jessie Li (jessielzh at gmail.com).
